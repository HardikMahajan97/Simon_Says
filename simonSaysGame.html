Simon Says Game

Rules for the game:

  • Press on start button to start the game
  • After pressing, out of the four, one box will flash white.
    Be attentive over which box does it so!
  • You click that box that flashed up and then after a second another box will flash up.
  • Now, you have to remember and click on all the previous boxes which flashed up in their original sequence and then click on the recent box that flashed up.
  • After every correct pattern, you level up!
  • If you forget the pattern and click on the wrong box, you lose!
    Hit start button again to restart the game.
  • You may start the game!
